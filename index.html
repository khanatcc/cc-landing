Common Questions

Do I need meditation experience?
+

No. You need maturity: 5-7 years of career work, some self-awareness, and a willingness to be clear with yourself about where you're stuck. Meditation helps, but it's not required. Psychedelic experience or other breakthrough work counts as practice.

What if I can't do 12 weeks right now?
+

We'll talk about that on your call. There's a once-per-week Foundation program for people who want the practice but need flexibility. But don't decide before you talk to someone—timing often becomes clearer in conversation.

How is this different from therapy or coaching?
+

Therapy works with your wounds. Coaching works with your goals. CC works with your capacity to see. We're not healing the past or optimizing performance — we're deconstructing the structure of how you experience reality itself.

How much does the 12-week bootcamp cost?
+

$2,450 for the complete 12-week intensive bootcamp. For context: a 10-day Vipassana retreat costs $0 but requires full-time commitment. A year of therapy costs $5,000-$10,000. A single ayahuasca ceremony costs $500-$1,500. We offer payment plans for people who are committed but need flexibility on timing.

What happens if I'm not ready?
+

We'll tell you directly. Not everyone is ready. The bootcamp requires a specific kind of maturity. If you're not there yet, we'll point you toward what might serve you better — or suggest you check in again in 6-12 months.

What's a transmission holder?
+

Someone who has completed the path and can point directly to what's true. In CC's case, Mei Cheng holds the lineage and leads the deepest work. The mentors train under him. This isn't a coaching program where everyone offers their personal perspective — it's a structured lineage with a realized teacher at the center.
